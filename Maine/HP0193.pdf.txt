This bill is a concept draft pursuant to Joint Rule 208.
This bill proposes to amend the current law dealing with recipients of Temporary
Assistance for Needy Families. The bill will require recipients of Temporary Assistance
for Needy Families to work with town officials and municipal officers to seek meaningful
employment and will require recipients of such benefits to work or volunteer in order to
continue to receive benefits.
