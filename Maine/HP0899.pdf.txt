1. Program established. The department shall administer a program to provide
employment-related transportation assistance to families receiving benefits under the
Temporary Assistance for Needy Families program under chapter 1053-B or the food

purposes.

4. Rulemaking. The department shall adopt rules to implement this section,

SUMMARY

23
24
25
26
27
28

This bill requires the Department of Health and Human Services to administer a
program to provide financial assistance to families receiving Temporary Assistance for
Needy Families benefits or statewide food supplement program benefits for motor vehicle
repairs, registration and inspection when the motor vehicle is needed for employment
purposes. Under the bill, the program must be supported with federal block grant funding
under the Temporary Assistance for Needy Families program.
