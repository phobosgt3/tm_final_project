21. Work search requirement. Before TANF assistance may be granted to an
applicant by the department, the applicant, if job ready as determined by the department,
must apply in writing for 3 separate advertised jobs and produce verifiable documentation
to the department of the applications. To satisfy this work search requirement, the job
applications may be submitted at any time from one week prior to the date of the
application to the department for TANF assistance to 2 weeks following that date.
